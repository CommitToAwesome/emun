What is a Model UN?

Think of it as the ultimate academic role-play. Students don't just learn about the world; they lead it. It is a structured simulation of the United Nations where students act as diplomats to solve global crises.

Public Oratory

Delegates deliver formal speeches, learning to communicate complex ideas under the pressure of a global audience.

Deep Research

Delegates master a "Position Paper" detailing their country's stance on items like e-commerce and health.

Consensus Building

The goal is not to "win," but to pass a Resolution. This teaches teamwork and diplomatic compromise.
